    WHO I AM

    Hi, I'm Riccardo, a young amateur photographer, I live in Portogruaro a small town in the province of Venice. Since I was a child I have always spent my holidays travelling. The discovery of new places inspired me to go on this new adventure. Landscapes, details, people, everything that I see through my eyes will be captured, frozen in time. The main goal of this project is to become a timecaspule for the future generations to see.

    WHAT I BRING

    To show the world my point of view, I always try to capture the uniqueness of my subject, spacing from nature, to architecture to people. Everything has a story to tell, even through images.

    EQUIPMENT

    For my project I choose a Nikon D3500, the right one to shoot a great and it is great to pick a photo on the ground. Another equipment for my project I use the DJI Mavic Mini2, it is a compact drone and I take it everywhere inside the backpack.

    AIR SECTION

    For the best land-photography or panoramic to the city, the mini2 is the best way to capture a moment. Everytime I see the videos or photo I feel like I’m up there to film or take a picture. Take a breath to see the world as a bird
